Husky Hub

Year: 2024


For my back-end web development course at UConn, the project was to make a Twitter clone using Express JS, Nunjucks templating language, and databases. I worked with two other Web Design majors on our Twitter Clone, rebranded as “Husky Hub.” The app allows users to create an account, post “woofs,” and delete their own “woofs.” There is also a direct message feature that is not functional, but we still designed the screen. I worked on the front-end, another person did the back-end, and the third person made a Figma design, which is shown in the picture. This advanced web development course was for seniors only and encompasses all of our design and coding knowledge learned in the major. My group received a 100 on this final project.
