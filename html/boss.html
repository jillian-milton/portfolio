The BOSS

Year: 2023

For my senior digital media capstone course, I worked with two other students to build a website and a marketing plan for a barber in Meriden, CT. The barber had a new invention called The BOSS, a portable device to clean hair from clippers. The right side of The BOSS blew the hair off the clippers, and the left side sanitized them by squirting cleaning liquid. The barber needed a website to promote his product. My group came up with color palettes and slogans. We chose a bold, masculine font and traditional barbershop colors: red, white, blue, and orange. We used original photography and video product demonstration in the Figma mockup and eventually onto the website. I also created a social media content calendar as part of a marketing strategy for The BOSS.
