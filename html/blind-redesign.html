Blind Pioneers

Year: 2022

This is a redesign of Blind Pioneers from the summer 2021 course. I improved the layout so that information can be accessed through various points of entry. For example, the home page now has featured articles that bring users to that respective article. Users can also click “Categories” and read an article or watch a video pertaining to that category. Out of the 12 categories, the most popular ones are, “Assistive Technology,” “Daily Living Videos,” “Education,” “K-12 Rights,” and “Blind People in the News.” I also changed the color palette from orange to navy blue and a bright lime green to better support my “pioneering/ space age” theme. The updated font also reflects a futuristic space theme. With these aesthetic and functional improvements to the original website complete, I hope to code the website and publish it again.
