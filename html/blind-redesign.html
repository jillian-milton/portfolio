Blind Pioneers

Year: 2022

In 2022, I redesigned Blind Pioneers, a website I originally created in 2021 using a Wix template with curated original content. I improved the layout and restructured the information so that topics could be readily found through various entry points. For example, the home page features articles that bring users to that respective article. Users can also click “Categories” and read an article or watch a video pertaining to that category. I also changed the color palette and fonts to better convey the explorer/ space age theme. With these aesthetic and functional improvements to the original website completed, I hope to code the website and publish it again in the future.
