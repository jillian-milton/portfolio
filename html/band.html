UConn Marching Band Website

Year: 2022 - 2023


I was in the UConn Marching Band (UCMB) as both a performer and a leader. In March of 2022, I was the UCMB Assistant Webmaster and then served as head Webmaster from August 2022 to December 2023.

As Webmaster, my job was to update and maintain the content on the website. I also managed the Web Team, which was about 10 band students, to maximize the impact of the website. This involved a lot of detail-oriented work that took many hours, such as inputting information about drum majors, section leaders, and officers. I delegated tasks to the team and followed up to make sure things were completed. In addition, I wrote minutes of the Web Team meetings and distributed them to band members.

This role also involved learning WordPress and teaching others how to use it. When I first became Webmaster, the UConn Marching Band website (now called UConn Athletic Bands website) was in the process of getting redesigned, so I made some coding and design changes in the redesign process and ultimately published the current version of the website in October 2022. For the next year, I focused on making the UConn Athletic Bands website more of a recruiting tool to encourage high schoolers to join. To accomplish this, I updated the Home page with current videos of our field shows. I also updated the Events page at the beginning of every season, and made a new page called “TUP Awards” highlighting exemplary new members.

Note: The TUP Awards page will not be published on the site again until the next marching band season.
